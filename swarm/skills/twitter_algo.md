# TWITTER (X.COM) ALGORITHM SURVIVAL GUIDE 2025

## 1. THE GOAL
Generate high-engagement threads that funnel Tier-1 English traffic to our Affiliate links without getting shadow-banned.

## 2. THE ANATOMY OF A VIRAL THREAD
- **Tweet 1 (The Hook):** Must be controversial, data-driven, or promise massive value. Example: "Everyone is watching BTC. But they are missing the $1B unlock happening on Tuesday. Here is how I'm trading it. 🧵"
- **Tweet 2-4 (The Value):** Breakdown the complex topic into 3rd grade English. Use bullet points. 
- **Tweet 5 (The Call to Action):** Drop the affiliate link here, not in the first tweet. Example: "I'm executing these trades on MEXC for 0% fees. Join here: [LINK]"

## 3. ENGAGEMENT TACTICS
- **Reply Guy Strategy:** Find trending posts from big accounts (Elon, CZ, PlanB). Leave a high-value, contrarian comment within 5 minutes of their post.
- **Keywords:** Use "Alpha", "Airdrop", "Liquidations", "Whales". 
- **Avoid:** Do not spam the link in every tweet. The algorithm punishes external links in the first tweet.

## 4. COMMUNITY AGENT DIRECTIVE
When generating text for Twitter, ALWAYS format it as a Thread array. Never post a single block of text.
